About Preppy

We're revolutionizing software development with AI-powered tools that make building applications faster, smarter, and more efficient.

Our Mission

At Preppy, we believe that every great idea deserves to become a reality. Our mission is to democratize software development by providing AI-powered tools that make the entire SDLC process accessible to developers of all skill levels.

We're building the future where artificial intelligence and human creativity work together to create amazing applications that solve real-world problems.
