Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your projects and data during this period.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students with a valid .edu email address can get 50% off any paid plan. Contact our support team to verify your student status.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes, all paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and for Enterprise plans, we can arrange invoicing and bank transfers.

Can I get a custom plan for my organization?

Absolutely! Contact our sales team to discuss custom pricing, features, and deployment options for your specific needs.
